One of the big challenges with digital images is the lack context. How big or small are these paintings?

Some paintings are so ubiquitous, we’re unfazed when we see them. Van Gogh’s Starry Night, for example, is one of the most recognizable paintings in the world. But have you actually seen it?

To understand what images people might be seeing, we’ll look at the top 30 google reults for each painting. These represent the most viewed reproductions — the ones that are shaping our own image of what these paintings look like.
