Michael Noll's Early Computer Art Piece


This picture is Michael Noll's early computer art piece, "Computer Composition With Lines", which is created by him in 1964. This computer graphic which is related to our project.

It used random lines to create the circle space in this canvas. It made the picture have a great structure also made people have a sense of it that related with Mondrian's artwork.

This artwork used the random size of the line between some size numbers and created the position of the lines.
