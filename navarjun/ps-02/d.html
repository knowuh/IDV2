Charles Csuri

  • Born on July 4, 1922
  • He is a World War 2 Vetran and was awarded the Bronze Star for heroism in the Battle of the Bulge.
  • Created his first computer art in 1964
  • Recoginised as the father of digital art and animation by Smithsonian(magazine)
  • Co-founder of Cranston/Csuri Productions - one of the world's first computer animation production companies
  • In 2000, for his lifetime achievements in the fields of digital art and computer animation he received:
    • Governor's Award for the Arts for the best individual artist
    • Ohio State University Sullivant Award
  • He is still a professor Emeritus at The Advanced Computing Center for Art and Design The Ohio State University
  • The above artwork is titled Designer Cheese
